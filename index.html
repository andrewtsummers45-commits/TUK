Our Story

TUK

From the frozen tundra of Green Bay, Wisconsin, TUK is a powerhouse original rock band carving out their place in the scene since their formation in 2021.

Drawing from the intensity of Metallica, the progressive edge of Gojira, and the anthemic spirit of Queen, TUK delivers a dynamic sound that demands your full attention.

Their songs span the full range of human experience — from good times on the town to the painful yet fulfilling journey of personal growth.
